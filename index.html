About Us

Conflicts transformed | Relationships restored | Communities living in justice and peace | Nations healed

Peacemakers, in search of radical solutions for conflict and violence, is working towards peace and stability, healing and rest, forgiveness and reconciliation. Equipping individuals, families, and civil society actors to nurture a culture of peace, justice, and environmental sustainability.

We strive to

  1. Prevent Violence, Promote Peace Education, Preserve Culture of Peace, Make Justice, Peace and Reconciliation as Popular Culture in our Communities.
  2. Bring about Conflict Transformation with Peacebuilding Skills Training, for multiple stakeholders including youth, civil society members & leaders and marginalized groups.
  3. Research on Peace & Conflict Studies and Document Peace Processes.
  4. Collaboratively Build Peace - Strategic Partnerships and Networking, building Peace Movement.
  5. Facilitate Healing & Restoration for individuals, families, groups, organizations, communities and nations.
  6. Work on Criminal Justice - Psycho-Socio Rehabilitation.
  7. Bring change on Environment Protection & Climate Action.

Project - PEHEL

A Criminal Justice Initiative to help individuals in prison reform and rebuild their lives.

In the beginning of 2016, we began to work with women prisoners with a goal to rehabilitate them and prepare them for reintegration with their families and society at large. The project since then has grown leaps and bounds and we are thankful for all who have made this possible.

In general, prisoners are viewed in fear as criminals or those requiring no mercy. But we forget they are humans, who often end-up in prison because of wrong handling of conflicts that turned violent or their life-circumstances culminating in an offense or a moment of losing self-control and committing violence, or even wrongly framed.
